Gnocchi

Gnocchi is simple to make with just three ingredients: mashed potato, flour, and egg. This recipe is one my family has used for generations.

Ingredients:

  • 2 potatoes, peeled
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg

Steps:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil
  2. Combine 1 cup mashed potato, flour, and egg in a large bowl. Knead until dough forms a ball
  3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
